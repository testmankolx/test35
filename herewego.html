Numbers - An ERC404 Project
Numbers is a collection of 10000 numbers enabled by ERC404, an experimental token standard.
Information:
  • ERC404: The ERC404 standard mixes the ERC20 and ERC721 standards together. This allows for native liquidity and fractionalization. The implementation strives to mix the two ERCs in a robust manner while minimizing tradeoffs.
  • ERC721?: The implementation is non-standard. Tokens are burned and minted repeatedly as per underlying fractional transfers. This part of the design is deliberate, with the goal of creating an NFT that has native fractionalization, liquidity, and encourages trading and engagement to obtain unique trait sets.
Project Goals:
  • Innovative Token Standard: Our primary goal is to develop a cutting-edge token standard that revolutionizes the way digital assets are represented and exchanged. Drawing inspiration from ERC404, we aim to create a versatile standard that seamlessly integrates the benefits of ERC20 and ERC721, fostering native liquidity and fractionalization while minimizing trade-offs.
  • Engagement and Interaction: We aspire to cultivate an ecosystem where token holders actively engage in trading and interaction. By implementing non-standard features akin to ERC404, such as repeated token minting and burning, our goal is to incentivize participation, encourage trading, and enhance user engagement within the platform.
  • Community Collaboration: Collaboration lies at the heart of our project. We seek to collaborate closely with our community, leveraging their insights and feedback to iterate and improve upon our token standard continuously. Through open communication channels and community-driven initiatives, we aim to foster a vibrant and inclusive ecosystem around our project.
  • Accessibility and Education: Education is key to widespread adoption. We are committed to providing accessible resources and educational materials to empower individuals and developers to understand and leverage the potential of our token standard. By lowering barriers to entry and promoting understanding, we aim to democratize access to decentralized finance and digital assets.
  • Ethical and Transparent Practices: Transparency and ethical practices are fundamental values guiding our project. We prioritize transparency in our operations, ensuring that our community is informed about project developments, governance decisions, and any changes to our token standard. Upholding ethical principles, we strive to build trust and credibility within the broader blockchain community.
